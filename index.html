Portfolio

Below is a collection of a few data science projects I have worked on recently. Click on the project title or image to read more!

Indeed Data Science Jobs

Data Science job postings in three cities - Toronto, New York City, and San Francisco - were scraped from Indeed. The objective was to analyze how job markets compare and what skills in data science are highly in demand. Techniques such as topic modeling and association rules mining were used to get insights about the job postings.

Stocks Performance Analysis

US stocks dataset with financial indicators was analyzed to determine performance trends. Financial indicators such as current ratio, debt-to-equity ratio, asset turnover, net profit margin, etc. were analyzed in the EDA. Performance was compared across different sectors and price variation over 4 years was analyzed to determine sectors for stocks that would have been a good buy.

Amazon Customer Reviews

3 million Amazon customer reviews were analyzed to identify patterns and trends. The key question that was being answered in the EDA was: What makes a customer review helpful? Key findings were that helpful reviews have a longer average word count, lower overall rating, neutral sentiment, and frequent usage of objective words.

Bike Sharing Demand

Built a predictive model to forecast use of a city bikeshare system. The features in the data include datetime, season, holiday, working day, weather, air temperature, humidity, and windspeed. A log linear regression model was built to predict the number of bike rentals and feature engineering was performed to improve model performance.
